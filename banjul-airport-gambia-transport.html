Traveling from Banjul International Airport

As you arrive at Banjul International Airport (BJL), located in Yundum, you're stepping into the vibrant heart of Gambia. Whether you're here for business or leisure, getting from the airport to your final destination is convenient, thanks to a variety of Banjul Airport taxi and transfer services.

Here are your main options:

  • Banjul Airport Taxi: Easily identifiable, taxis are a straightforward way to reach your destination. They are available directly outside the arrivals hall.
  • Private Gambia Airport Transfer: For a more comfortable and hassle-free experience, consider pre-booking a private transfer. Vehicles range from sedans for solo travelers or couples to larger vehicles for families and groups.
  • Car Rentals: If you prefer the flexibility of driving yourself, several car rental agencies operate within the airport.
